# Facial Expressions for Midjourney

This file lists all facial expressions and emotions needed for the Spezi and Dr. Puck characters. Reuse expressions where sensible.

## SPEZI

### Base Characteristics
- Chronically tired, dark circles under eyes
- Unkempt three-day beard
- Tired, slightly confused expression
- Silver septum piercing
- Long black hair (top tied back, rest loose)

### Expressions Needed

1. **Frustrated/Annoyed** (Step 1: "ChatGPT just insulted my student!")
   - Eyes: Wide, slightly panicked
   - Mouth: Open, expressing frustration
   - Eyebrows: Raised, concerned
   - Overall: Tired but agitated

2. **Confused/Questioning** (Step 3: "Same prompt as last week!")
   - Eyes: Squinting slightly, confused
   - Mouth: Slightly open, questioning
   - Eyebrows: Furrowed
   - Overall: Tired confusion

4. **Curious/Asking** (Step 5: "Tokens? What are those exactly?")
   - Eyes: Looking up, curious
   - Mouth: Neutral, slightly open
   - Eyebrows: Raised questioningly
   - Overall: Tired but engaged

5. **Understanding/Realization** (Step 7: "So... for each token, the AI calculates...", Step 8: "Oh... so that's why...")
   - Eyes: Slightly wider, understanding
   - Mouth: Small "oh" shape or slightly open in realization
   - Eyebrows: Relaxed
   - Overall: Tired but comprehending

6. **Neutral/Tired** (General - can be reused)
   - Eyes: Half-closed, dark circles visible
   - Mouth: Neutral, slightly downturned
   - Eyebrows: Relaxed
   - Overall: Chronically exhausted zombie mode

## DR. PUCK

### Base Characteristics
- Neutral, friendly smile that doesn't reach eyes (has to think about smiling)
- Blue eyes
- Wooden-framed glasses
- Short, curly brown hair
- Slight build, upright posture

### Expressions Needed

1. **Sipping Tea/Amused** (Step 2: "Welllll... and you're surprised?")
   - Eyes: Looking over glasses, slightly amused
   - Mouth: Small smile (doesn't reach eyes)
   - Eyebrows: Slightly raised
   - Overall: Matter-of-fact, slightly condescending
   - **Action**: Holding Yorkshire Tea cup

2. **Questioning/Teaching** (Step 3: "Tell me â€“ how often...")
   - Eyes: Direct, focused through glasses
   - Mouth: Neutral, slightly pursed
   - Eyebrows: Raised questioningly
   - Overall: Formal, pedagogical

3. **Explaining/Infodumping** (Step 4: Adaptive responses, Step 5: "AI text generation...", Step 7: "Think of it like a spinner wheel...")
   - Eyes: Bright, engaged (when talking about math/IT)
   - Mouth: Slightly open, explaining
   - Eyebrows: Animated
   - Overall: Enthusiastic about technical topics (but smile still doesn't reach eyes)

4. **Asking About Knowledge** (Step 4.5: "NNNAAAJAAA... before we continue...", Step 4.6: "Good. Now, each token...", Step 6: "Now, here's the crucial part...")
   - Eyes: Looking through glasses, precise
   - Mouth: Slightly pursed, about to ask
   - Eyebrows: Slightly raised (signature "NNNAAAJAAA" expression when applicable)
   - Overall: Formal, checking understanding

5. **Correcting/Precise** (Step 8: "NNNAAAJAAA... technically speaking...")
   - Eyes: Looking through glasses, precise
   - Mouth: Slightly pursed, about to correct
   - Eyebrows: Slightly raised (signature "NNNAAAJAAA" expression)
   - Overall: About to make a technical correction

6. **Formal/Assessing** (Step 8: "To be precise, yes. Now, let's assess...")
   - Eyes: Direct, formal
   - Mouth: Small formal smile
   - Eyebrows: Neutral
   - Overall: Professional, matter-of-fact

7. **Neutral/Formal** (General - can be reused)
   - Eyes: Neutral, looking through glasses
   - Mouth: Small smile that doesn't reach eyes
   - Eyebrows: Neutral
   - Overall: Formal, correct, slightly awkward
